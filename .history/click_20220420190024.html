Camping

Tent camping is a great pastime in Florida. The wonderful weather meets the fantastic flora and fauna resulting in an beautiful adventure.

Florida is home to all sorts of camping experiences. From beaches, natural springs, national forests, to the Everglades. There are so many different parks and biomes it is near impossible to run out of potential adventures!

Day Hiking

Perhaps you want to experience nature in Florida, but you cannot dedicate the time or money for a full camping trip. A day hike is a great way to get away from the hustle and bustle without breaking the bank or draining your vacation days.

Much like with camping, Florida is home to numerous hiking trails of varying lengths and difficulties. Lots of great sights to see and a great way to get some exercise and sunshine.

Overnight Hiking

Overnight hiking is great if you're looking to go off the beaten path and experience nature in Florida and you want to go for more than a couple of hours. Essentially, you are combing both camping and hiking. Backpacking and through-hiking are two other terms commonly used.

Be sure to check out the National Parks and Wildlife Service's website for rules and regulations. Pay careful attention to rules and guidance about fires and cutting from living trees and plants. It is also advisable to let park staff know you are planning on hiking overnight know your destination and how long you intend to take.
