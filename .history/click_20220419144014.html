Camping

Tent camping is a great pastime in Florida. The wonderful weather meets the fantastic flora and fauna resulting in an beautiful adventure.

Florida is home to all sorts of camping experiences. From beaches, natural springs, national forests, to the Everglades. There are so many different parks and biomes it is near impossible to run out of potential adventures!

Day Hiking

Much like with camping, Florida is home to numerous hiking trails of varying lengths and difficulties. Perhaps you want to experience nature in Florida, but you cannot dedicate the time or money for a full camping trip. A day hike is a great way to get away from the hustle and bustle without breaking the bank or draining your vacation days.
